Launch of Science Publishing Group's
new website
The new website of the Science Publishing Group went live on 25 March, 2014.
The launch of the new website, which offers quick and easy access to essential information on SciencePG, is part of the organisation’s ongoing efforts to enhance the quality and availability of information to members and visitors worldwide.

The website boasts a modern, colourful design and is divided into three sections: journals, books, conferences. Each page provides detailed information on all three aspects of SciencePG’s business.

The website offers three special features: information of discounted journals, information of special issues and upcoming books. The website’s user-friendly nature provides users with more efficient access to the organisation’s resources.

Special Issues
In an effort to promote the journal more widely, Science Publishing Group is inviting potential Guest Editors to submit proposals for Special Issues to be published in 2014. Previous Special Issue proposers are encouraged to submit revised proposals for consideration.

Now, we have almost forty special issues, for detailed information, please visit: http://www.sciencepublishinggroup.com/specialissuellist.aspx

If you are interested in one of the special issue, please join us. Being our Lead Guest Editor, You will be entitled following benefits:
1. You can keep yourself up-to-date while checking and suggesting the changes in articles.
2. You can cooperate with a group of experienced researchers from all over the world.
3. You can get a certificate from SciencePG.
4. You can publish two articles for free.
5. Except for the above two articles, SciencePG will give 80% discounts for your other articles.

To submit a proposal of the special issue, please send your proposal to special.issues@sciencepublishinggroup.com
To join as the Lead Guest Editor or submit an article for the special issue, please join us via online system http://submission.sciencepublishinggroup.com/login.aspx
